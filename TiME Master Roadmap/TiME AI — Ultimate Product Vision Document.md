> ## Core Prompt

"Design an AI-powered time management and productivity system that eliminates the most critical and costly inefficiencies faced by individuals and teams globally — including context-switching, procrastination, calendar overload, decision fatigue, and fragmented workflows. The system must feature a radically intuitive UI, built from the ground up using futuristic interaction patterns, and feel like a personal COO embedded in every device. It should blend time tracking, scheduling, focus coaching, prioritization, and accountability into a unified experience — more natural, proactive, and intelligent than anything currently available (Motion, Notion, Sunsama, ClickUp, etc.). The goal is to unlock trillions in lost productivity by reinventing how humans manage time at the cognitive, operational, and behavioral levels."

> ## UI/UX Vision

- **Spatial Time Interface**: Rethink calendars — imagine a timeline that you can interact with like a 3D canvas.
- **Voice + Chat Native**: Speak or chat to manage your life: "Reschedule all meetings for deep work mode."
- **Dynamic Layouts**: UI adapts to time of day, energy level, and focus state.
- **Emotion-Aware Design**: Color schemes, animation, and typography change based on productivity patterns.
- **Zero-Friction Input**: Tasks can be added via voice, AI summarization, or direct input — frictionless and fast.
- **Live Behavioral Feedback**: Real-time response to distractions, like "Hey, you're off task for 7 minutes — want to resume deep work?"
